GeoCHOROS is a spatial research group powered by the Geography Lab at National Technical University of Athens, Greece. The group focuses on research and education with regards to City Science, Urban and Transport Geography, Urban Sustainability, Planning Healthier Mobility Systems & Communities, Urban Analytics, Geospatial Analysis, as well as Geographic Information Science.
